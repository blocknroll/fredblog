First Flight Off the Top!

By Fred Block

8/22/2019

I'm pretty excited about today. If the conditions are right, this might be the day I get to fly off the top of the hill for the first time. First solo!

Doug and I both were ready to launch off the top yesterday evening. But just as we were about to go, the wind died down and it was a no-go. No worries. No need to rush it. Our time will come again.

And here it is. This evening's session is starting to look good...

Launches looked great. Let's keep an eye on this...

Looking good. People staying up

On one hand, I'm super excited that things are lining up and its looking like I might get to fly. On the other hand, I'm bummed Doug isn't here to get some with us. Next time, Douglas!

Beautiful night for a flight, wouldn't you say?

I'm ready to go. Kiting feels good and the wing is easy to control.

Chris walks over super casually, and gives me the nod. We run over the checklist, two three couple pointers, and check the radios.

Chris smiles.
"You good?"
"I'm good."
"Alright buddy, let's do this!"
All smiles.

I turn and start toward the edge. "Looks good buddy, no traffic, its your time, send it."

All smiles.

"Any move worth making,
is worth making
with your eyes first."
-Chris Santacroce
